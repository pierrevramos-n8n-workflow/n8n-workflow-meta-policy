Data Deletion Instructions

Application: n8n workflow
Last update: February 9, 2026

Data Deletion Request

If you wish to request the deletion of your data associated with the n8n workflow application:

  1. Send an email to: pierrervramos@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request – n8n workflow
  3. Include:
    • Your full name
    • The email associated with your Facebook account
    • The Facebook Page name (if applicable)

Processing Time

All verified data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receipt.

Automatic Removal

Users may remove the application at any time via:
Facebook → Settings → Business Integrations → Remove “n8n workflow”.

Once removed, access tokens will be invalidated.
